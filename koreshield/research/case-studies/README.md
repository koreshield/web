# Sanitized Case Studies

These case studies are sanitized, product-safe walkthroughs of the kinds of
enterprise attacks KoreShield is intended to stop.

They serve three purposes:

- support investor and customer conversations
- anchor product claims in realistic workflows
- provide reusable examples for demos and validation

Included:

- `salesforce-indirect-prompt-injection.md`
- `hubspot-support-note-exfiltration.md`
